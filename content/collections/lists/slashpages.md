# slashes

this is a **/slashes** page. a [/slashes](https://flamedfury.com/slashes/) page is an index of all my “[slash pages](https://slashpages.net/)”. slash pages are common pages you can add to your website, usually with a standard, root-level slug like [/now](https://jubilancy.github.io/now), [/about](https://jubilancy.github.io/about), or [/uses](https://jubilancy.github.io/uses). they tend to describe the individual behind the site and are distinguishing characteristics of the [indieweb](https://jubilancy.github.io/indieweb).
