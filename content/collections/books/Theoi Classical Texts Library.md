**The Theoi Classical Texts Library** is a collection of translations of works of ancient Greek and Roman literature. The theme of the library is classical mythology and so the selection consists primarily of ancient poetry, drama and prose accounts of myth.

---

**The Theoi Greek Mythology Website** ([www.theoi.com](https://www.theoi.com/)) was compiled several years before the creation of this online library and in many cases quotes from different translations. It also draws on a considerably wider selection of classical literature. For more information on that site's content please refer to the [Theoi Greek Mythology Site Bibliography](https://www.theoi.com/Bibliography.html).
