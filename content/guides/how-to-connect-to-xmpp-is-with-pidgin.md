---
title: "how to connect to xmpp is with pidgin"
---

*If you haven’t yet registered an account go here: [xmpp.is/register](https://xmpp.is/register/)*

If you don’t have Pidgin you can get it here at [pidgin.im](https://pidgin.im/), it is the most common Jabber/XMPP client and works great. We also recommend you use OTR which will ensure your conversations can’t be deciphered even if our systems are compromised. You should go here [xmpp.is/docs/general/otr](https://xmpp.is/docs/general/otr/) for our guide after you’ve signed up for an account.

When you have installed Pidgin, open it and go to *Accounts > Manage Accounts* and press “Add”. This should show up and fill out the details to connect to your recently created account. This is an example:

[![pidgin1](https://xmpp.is/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/pidgin1.png)](https://xmpp.is/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/pidgin1.png)

After filling out the above with your details, press “Add”. It should now connect, and you will be on your new XMPP account!
