It looks like you’re gathering the final pieces for your **Obsidian-based digital garden**. Since you’re already managing your vault and syncing it with tools like **Astro** or **Quartz**, these "Importer" and "Merger" plugins are great for consolidating all those scattered notes and highlights into one place.

Here are the direct GitHub/Obsidian directory links for the plugins you listed, organized by category.
